Our philosophy:

Let's be real: our connected devices are pulling us farther apart. Friends or family with whom you once shared real genuine moments, are trapped behind a 3x5 inch display. That's not cool, so we decided to use technology to counteract social isolation. Pulse aims to repurpose the nature of social networking, communal connectivity, and the internet as a whole, to form and deepen human relationships.


Once signed in, you will have all the tools you need to connect with your community.
